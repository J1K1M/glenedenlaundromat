About Us

Welcome to Glen Eden Laundromat, where we provide
the best laundry care and service in West Auckland.
Open 7 days a week, we offer our services to local customers and
also provide free pick up and delivery for commercial laundry services.
Just come in, drop off your laundry, let us know
what services you would like and pick it up later, it's that easy.

Our Service

Wash

Washing your laundry with cold water and high quality laundry powder and softner for those that need it.

Dry

Carefully dry your laundry at high temperature (unless otherwise requested) for the requested amount of time.

Dry Cleaning

Dry cleaning agents, leave your dry cleaning with us and come pick it up when it's ready!

Alteration

Alteration agents, drop off your clothes that need alteration
